Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("ID Scanner") handles user data.

1. Data Collection

We do not store or retain personal identity information from scanned IDs.

All ID scanning is processed locally on the device unless otherwise stated.

2. Camera Usage

The app uses your device camera solely to scan and process identification documents.

No images or data are stored or shared by default.

3. Analytics and Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools to improve app performance and user experience:

  • Firebase – for analytics, performance monitoring, and crash reporting
  • Amplitude – for understanding user interactions and app usage
  • Adapty – for managing subscriptions and in-app purchases

These services may collect anonymized data such as:

  • Device information
  • Usage data
  • App interactions

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal identity data with third parties.

Collected analytics data is used solely for improving the application.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and ensure secure processing.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to request information about your data.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at:
